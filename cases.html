Smart targeting
Challenge:
Direct marketing campaigns are expensive. Wrong targeting can damage your brand. Recipients are tired of getting too much spam and might opt out. Traditional techniques are not effective, resulting in lower response rates, which leads to higher acquisition costs.
Solution:
Artificial intelligence solutions can dramatically boost accuracy for identifying prospects with the highest probabilities for conversion for a certain marketing campaign so that you only would contact those with a high likelihood to respond.
Why Neuton:
Neuton can bring the power of artificial intelligence to the fingertips of every marketer since it does not require any special knowledge or training. Besides Neuton is as effective with small sets of data as well as with large datasets, making it available for any Small and Medium size businesses fewer historical data to derive value out of their statistics.
